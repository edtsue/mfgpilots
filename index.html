What is a pilot?

✦

A working artifact, not a deck

Every pilot is a real, interactive site. The audience doesn't watch a mock — they operate the concept, change the inputs, and watch the creative respond.

◎

Real signals, live AI

Context in — geography, moment, audience, live moments — and AI composites the asset on the spot. The pitch proves the pipeline, not just the picture.
